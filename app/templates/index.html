Phishing Email Simulator

About the Project

This phishing email simulator is designed to help organizations train their employees to recognize and avoid phishing attempts. By simulating realistic phishing scenarios, users can learn to identify suspicious emails without any real security risk.

Key Features:

  • Create customizable email templates
  • Target specific departments or individuals
  • Track open, click, and submission rates
  • Generate detailed reports
  • Schedule campaigns for future deployment
