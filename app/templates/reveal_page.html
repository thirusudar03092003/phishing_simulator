Why did we do this?

Phishing attacks are one of the most common ways that cybercriminals gain access to sensitive information and systems. By conducting simulated phishing tests, we can help you recognize and avoid real phishing attempts.

Signs this was a phishing attempt:
  • The email created a sense of urgency
  • It asked for your credentials
  • The URL did not match our official website
  • There were subtle grammar or design issues

What to do in the future:

  • Always check the sender's email address carefully
  • Hover over links before clicking to see the actual URL
  • Be suspicious of urgent requests for personal information
  • When in doubt, contact IT directly using known contact information
  • Report suspicious emails to your IT department
